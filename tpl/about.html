About WaterTOS

WaterTOS is a project that aims to simplify TOS documents and portray them in a creative way. We aim to make the arduous task of reading TOS documents flow like water, and in turn, fix the ignorance of legal documents

Why "WaterTOS"?

We aim to make reading TOS documents "flow like water" and make them as transparent as possible. In all honesty, it was an accident. That story's for another time.

How does it work?

We crawl websites on demand and scrape links which appear "legally". The data in these links is then feed through the APIs at Alyien and a simple content extractor of our own. After this is complete, the text is analysed to find frequently appearing words.

But what if a document is updated?

Document data lives in our database for up to a week. After a week is over, the next request for the document will prompt a new download of the document (which refreshes votes). If this fails then we will keep trying every subsequent request for the page.
